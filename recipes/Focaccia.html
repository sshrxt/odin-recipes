Smoked Tomato and Mozzarella on Focaccia

Who else loves the starbucks tomato and mozzerla on focaccia sandwhich? I know i'm not the only one! In this recipie, we will be recreating this sandwhich with a unique twist: the tomatoes in this sandwhich will me smoked and spicy!

  • Tomatoes
  • Smoked tobasco sauce
  • Focaccia Bread
  • Pesto
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Arugula
  • Olive Oil
  1. Cut your tomatoes to a desired slice and lay them out on a frying pan
  2. Coat the tomatoes with smoked tobasco sauce, olive oil, and salt
  3. Bake until desired consistency is achieved
  4. Meanwhile, apply pesto on both sides of focaccia bread
  5. In a bowl, mix arugula and Balsamic vinegar until satisfied
  6. Put the Balsamic coated arugula on top of the pesto
  7. Cut fresh mozzarella into slices and put on top of arugula
  8. Then, take out ur tomatoes and put the tomatoes on top of the mozzarella cheese
  9. Put your constructed sandwhich in a panini press and wait until the sandwhich is done
  10. Serve with fresh juice and enjoy!
