Pasta alla Vodka w/o Vodka!

Who doesn't like pasta? This recipe models a very popular pasta dish that is famous for its vodka sauce. However, in this recipie, we will not be using vodka!

  • Butter
  • Red or White Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Paste
  • Heavy Cream
  • Pasta water
  • Rigatoni Pasta
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Oregano, Basil, Salt
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  1. Bring a large pot of heavily salted water to a rolling boil over high heat. Once boiling, add pasta, stir to prevent sticking, and cook
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil until shimmering
  3. Add the onion and crushed garlic with sold and cook until the onion is translucent and garlic is lightly browned
  4. Add chili Flakes
  5. Add 2 tablespoons of tomato paste over medium heat and cook for about 2 minutes, until the paste darkens and becomes sticky
  6. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the cream, stir well!
  7. Drain the pasta, and add some pasta water into the skillet until desired sauce consistency is met
  8. Add Parmesan cheese and toss to combine
  9. Portion the pasta into bowls, garnish with some basil and parmesan, and enjoy!
