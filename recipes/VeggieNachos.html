Veggie Nachos

Whether it be a messy yet lovely dinner with your date or a gameday staple, everyone agrees on the importance of nachos. By following this recipe, you will be one step closer into impressing your loved ones!

  • Black Beans
  • Nacho Cheese Sauce
  • Taco Bell Seasoning
  • Sour Cream
  • Guacamole
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Jalapenos
  • Olive oil
  • Chipotle adobo peppers
  • Tortilla Chips
  1. Dice onions and jalapenos and satue them on a frying pan with olive oil
  2. Blend chipotle adobo peppers and mix taco bell seasoning into them
  3. Once onions and jalapenos are caralmized, add the blended adobo peppers
  4. Let the mixture cook for 5 minutes on medium heat
  5. Now wash ur black beans, and slowly add in the black beans onto the pan
  6. On a plate, set up ur desired amount of nacho chips
  7. Add ur smoked Black Beans onto the chips
  8. Add warmed Nacho Cheese
  9. Add Sour Cream, diced Onions, Tomatoes, and Jalapenos
  10. Add Guacamole and a slice of lime
  11. Dabble some hot sauce on the nachos
  12. Enjoy!
